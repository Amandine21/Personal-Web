About Me

>

When I was a child, I have always been fascinated with how the Universe works. I had many interests growing up, and I found art, science, and history to be my favorite subjects. The computer has been one of the most impactful tools ever derived from man, and many aspects of our everyday lives rely on this tool. I've worked my way to get into a really good engineering university known as Texas A&M, and I am currently majoring in Computer Engineering. I decided to become an engineer because I view engineering as one of the most creative arts out there, and the computer engineering discipline can be used in a variety of applications. My mission is to use what I learn to contribute to mankind's innovation and make the world of tomorrow a better place for all life. With a deep passion for understanding the mysteries of the universe and a strong foundation in STEM, I am excited to explore the possibilities of merging my interests and unlocking new frontiers of knowledge.
